Import external images in posts and pages from its original external sources or Web Archive if original source is unavailable.

== Description ==
Archivarix External Images Importer will scan your posts and pages for external urls in src/srcset attribute for all img tags. Based on configured settings it can:

* Download images from their external locations
* Download images from Internet Archive (Wayback Machine)

You can choose what to do with images that could not be retrieved from external sources.

For further information and instructions please see  [Archivarix WP Plugin](https://en.archivarix.com/wordpress/)
